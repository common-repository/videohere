A plugin to integrates VideoHere with your WordPress blog.

== Description ==

Marketers want to use video and you like to make marketers happy. With VideoHere, video features designed for marketers will enable your clients to use and track video content directly within your application quickly and easily. You can integrate video features in minutes with no upfront costs, so there's no need to spend lots of time and money building and maintaing video expertise yourself. Instead you can focus on how video impacts client success.

The VideoHere player enables a rich viewing experience for viewers and promotes viral distribution of videos with sharing and embed options. With VideoHere analytics, your users will know what's happening with their video content. They'll be able to measure viewership and engagement right next to your metrics.

The optional user acceptance feature allows users to signup and agree to terms directly within your application.

This WordPress plugin allows Webtrends customers to:
*  Add a VideoHere button to the editor in administration, providing them access to insert their video
*  Allows clients to alternatively connect to their <a href="http://www.backlight.tv">Backlight</a> account.

== Installation ==

1. You MUST have an active VideoHere account in order to use the plugin
1. Upload the `videohere` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to `Settings/VideoHere` and enter your VideoHere API key

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Does this plugin allow access to all my VideoHere Analytics =

Yes, you can view your analytics in VideoHere by clicking on the Analytics tab when you click on the VideoHere button.
